# Appliances Energy Prediction Project

Analysed household energy consumption using the **Appliances Energy Prediction** dataset (UCI ML Repository). Applied machine learning and time series techniques to uncover patterns, predict usage, and explore environmental factors affecting energy consumption.

---

## Objective
- Identify key factors influencing appliance energy use.
- Build predictive models for energy consumption.
- Explore patterns and clusters in the data.
- Forecast short-term energy demand.

---

## Dataset
- **Source:** UCI Machine Learning Repository  
- **Size:** 19,735 rows × 29 features  
- **Features:** Appliance energy, indoor/outdoor temperature, humidity, lights, timestamps  
- **Type:** Mostly continuous numeric data; time series

---

## Techniques & Tools
- **Regression:** Random Forest (best), SVR, Decision Tree, Polynomial Regression  
- **Clustering:** K-Means, Hierarchical Clustering  
- **Dimensionality Reduction:** PCA  
- **Time Series Forecasting:** ARIMA, SARIMA  
- **Tools:** Python, Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib, Seaborn  

---

## Key Results
- **Regression:** Random Forest achieved **R² = 0.37**, lowest MSE → best predictive accuracy  
- **Clustering:** K-Means scalable and interpretable; PCA improved cluster separation  
- **Forecasting:** SARIMA outperformed ARIMA in capturing daily energy cycles  
- **Insights:** Energy use influenced by both indoor and outdoor environmental factors; strong daily consumption patterns

---

## Conclusion & Skills Highlighted
- Data cleaning & preprocessing for large time series datasets  
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and feature insights  
- Machine Learning: Regression, Clustering, Dimensionality Reduction  
- Time Series Forecasting: ARIMA & SARIMA  
- Practical application: energy usage prediction, load planning, environmental analysis
